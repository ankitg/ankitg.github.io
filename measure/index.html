Lehenga Choli

Your Height*

How to measure Your Height

Stand with your feet slightly apart and your back to a wall. Measurement from the floor to the top of your head.

Around Bust*

How to measure Around Bust

Measure over the fullest part of the bust and across the widest part of the back. The measuring tape should go horizontally all around your body. (Keep the tape measure comfortably snug, not tight. Measure to the nearest Inch/Centimeter).

Around Above Waist*

How to measure Around Above Waist

To locate your waistline, tie a narrow string around the waist and let it settle into the natural waistline as you bend to the left and to the right. Take the measurement along the string line.

Around Waist*

How to measure Around Waist

Measure around 1 inch (2.5 centimeters) above your navel. (Keep the tape measure comfortably snug, not tight). Measure to the nearest inch.

Choli Length*

How to measure Choli Length

Measure length from the shoulder point to your desired lenght like shown in the image

Shoulder*

How to measure Shoulder

Hold the tape at the shoulder point. Measure total width from left shoulder to right shoulder across the top spinal bone. The total length in inch is your full shoulder width.

Around Arm Hole*

How to measure Around Arm Hole

Run the tape under the arm, pulling comfortably, and up to the shoulder point again in front. The total measurement is your armhole circumference

Sleeve Loose*

How to measure Sleeve Loose

Measure the middle of biceps level of your arm keeping the hand relaxed.

Sleeve Length*

How to measure Sleeve Length

The sleeve length of a blouse is usually a matter of personal taste and comfort level. (We recommend select As Shown in Image)

Front Neck Deep*

How to measure Front Neck Deep

Measure from the top of the shoulder, that is your neck joint, and measure till your cleavage, to the point where you want your blouse neck to end.

Back Neck Deep*

How to measure Back Neck Deep

Measure from the neck joint to the center of your back and choose how deep you would like the back of the blouse to be.

Choli Closing*

How to measure Choli Closing

Select closing type from Back Hook, Front Hook or Side Zip

Choli Type*

How to measure Choli Type

Select which type of choli you want to make, With Pads or Without Pads.

Around Hips*

How to measure Around Hips

Measure over an undergarment at the point where you protrude the most. Measure around the fullest part of your hips, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.

Waist Type*

How to measure Waist Type

Select Fully Elastic or Drawstring

Waist Circumference*

How to measure Waist Circumference

Measure on the waist where you normally wear your pant or skirt.

Lehenga Length*

How to measure Lehenga Length

Hold the tape at the waist point (wearing position). Measure down to your heel.

Heels*

How to measure Heels

If you want to wear the lehenga with heels, include the height of heels.
